Folktale Contest is a month long story writing contest in the month of November. This is a contest running parallel to the NaNoWriMo event, So you can take part in both! Folktale is a collaborative story telling platform so you are not really writing big novels here. Instead you are writing samll story parts to which other people can contribute to. And you can do the same as well! And the writer with the most popular stories wins!

How to participate?

  1. Log in to Folktale anytime in the month of November.
  2. Write your own stories, contribute to already started stories during November.
  3. Its the likes on your story parts that count. The participent with the most number of likes per chunk wins!
