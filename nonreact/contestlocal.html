at Folktale you can:

The Folktale Contest is a story writing contest. Entry is free! It runs parallel to the 2020 NaNoWriMo event, so you can take part in both!

Because Folktale is a collaborative story telling platform, contributions are much smaller than an entire novel. Instead you're writing small story parts, to which other people can contribute.

Use it as a way to:

  • relax from your novel
  • meet other writers
  • get inspired by interesting tales

How to participate:

  1. - Log in to Folktale anytime in the month of November.
  2. - During the month, you can write your own stories, or contribute to already started stories.
  3. - It's the likes on your contributions that count. The participant with the highest ratio of likes to contributions over the course of the month wins! You can share the contest via social media to get more votes from friends!
